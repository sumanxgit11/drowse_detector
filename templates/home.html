How It Works

  1. 1
    Face Detection

    MediaPipe Face Mesh locates the driver's face in each frame.

  2. 2
    Landmark Mapping

    468 facial landmark points are predicted around the face and eyes.

  3. 3
    EAR Calculation

    Eye Aspect Ratio computed from 6 eye landmarks per eye.

  4. 4
    Alert Trigger

    EAR < 0.22 for 20+ consecutive frames → drowsy alert fires.
