EAR for Drowsiness

Monitors 6 key landmarks around the eyes. An EAR below 0.22 indicates closed eyelids. Triggered after 20 frames.

🥱

MAR for Yawning

Calculates the Mouth Aspect Ratio (MAR) using 4 inner lip landmarks. An MAR above 0.50 detects wide yawning motions.

🔄

Head Pose for Distraction

Analyzes the 3D facial transformation matrix to extract Pitch and Yaw. Detects when the driver drops their head or looks away from the road.
